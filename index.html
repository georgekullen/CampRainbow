Camp Rainbow

A safe fun place where campers have an opportunity to learn new skills, grow a lifetime of friendships, connect with nature, and develop a sense of independence that can only come from being away from home. 

Located in Schwenksville, Pennsylvania along the Perkiomen creek, Camp Rainbow offers 18 acres of beautiful open space. We have 6 cabins for the campers, a large air conditioned kitchen and cafeteria, pool, low ropes, gaga pit, boating, a large covered pavilion and much more.


Through a variety of fun, adventure-based problem solving and team building experiences Campers will discover their leadership talents and experience the power of respect, responsibility, and healthy relationships. Additionally, campers become knowledgeable about mindfulness, healthy living as they participate in discussions about nutrition, exercise, stress, conflict resolution, bullying prevention. Some of the activities of camp include swimming, arts and crafts, archery, hiking on trails, row boating, basketball, fishing, campfires, skits and storytelling, nature study and adventure education programs (team building and low ropes). 
Camp Rainbow seeks to empower and inspire children to become self-sustaining adults.

Camp Rainbow and Friends values and uplifts the dignity and diversity of all people and does not discriminate or permit discrimination on the basis of actual or perceived age, race, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, education, ability or gender identity in our programs, activities or employment practices. It is part of our values, priorities and the policy of our organization that discrimination in any form such as but not limited to bullying, abusive language or actions, harassment or exclusion will not be tolerated toward our campers, parents/guardians, staff or volunteers. It is always our intention to provide a safe, welcoming and supportive space to our community but encourage communication if you find that this has not been the case.
